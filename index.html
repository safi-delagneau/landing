Who is he ?

Kevin Bonnet, known by his stage name Keen'V (pronounced [kinve]), is a French singer-songwriter. He was born on January 31, 1983 in Rouen, Seine-Maritime. He first gained recognition in 2008 with his song "À l'horizontale" ("Horizontally"). In 2011, he achieved mainstream success with the song "J'aimerais trop" ("I'd Like Too Much"). He has released ten albums, six of which have been certified platinum.
